The Bitter Reach is a harsh and unforgiving land, filled with danger at every turn. It's a place where only the strong survive, and those who wish to thrive must be willing to take risks and make sacrifices. The region is full of ancient ruins left behind by the winter elves, as well as hidden ice caves and settlements clinging precariously to the coastlines.

The Wake of the Winter King campaign will see your characters delving deep into this frozen wasteland in search of treasure and glory. Along the way, they'll encounter a variety of key players with their own motivations and goals, including merchants, settlers, outlaws, and more.

As you prepare to guide your players through this perilous landscape, be sure to read up on the various adventure sites detailed in Chapter 12, as well as the treasure types and other valuable items that can be found throughout the Bitter Reach. Remember, treasures should be balanced by danger, so don't be afraid to make encounters challenging for your players.

Good luck, and may your characters find fortune and fame in the frozen wilds of the Bitter Reach!
