SETTING - Barrowmaze is an exploration-style megadungeon located in the Duchy of Aerik, where the village of Helix resides. It's designed to begin with first-level characters and continue through mid and high-level play.

STARTING LOCATION - The adventure begins in the isolated village of Helix. Key establishments include: the Mercenary Guild, Turgen's Trade Goods, the Shrine of St. Ygg, and the Rosy Quartz Jeweler and Money Lender. NPCs include Barrelgut (the blacksmith), Osen (the Guildmaster), Brother Othar (Level 5 Cleric), Cella (Level 2 Cleric), Gamdar (Level 2 Half-Orc Cleric), Harnold Huwen Reginald Huffnpuff (the jeweler and money lender), and Hendon (the miller).

KEY NPCs - Barrelgut can craft unique non-magical dungeoneering items and equipment. Osen, the Guildmaster of the Mercenary Guild in Helix, recruits men-at-arms, henchmen, porters, torch-bearers, and guides for adventurers exploring Barrowmaze. Brother Othar (Level 5 Cleric), Cella (Level 2 Cleric), and Gamdar (Level 2 Half-Orc Cleric) oversee the Shrine of St. Ygg.

KEY LOCATIONS - The major areas in Barrowmaze include: Mound #7, Mound #12, Mound #16, Mound #20, Mound #27, Mound #35, Mound #36, and Mound #42.

RUMORS - Rumors circulating around Helix include statements about mutated people living in the Barrowmaze, all entrances to the maze being trapped, tomb-robbers recently returning with a valuable silver chalice, powerful evil warriors using it as a hideout, elven maidens freed from there, and piles of magical items found in the southern crypts.

MAJOR TREASURES - The Barrow Mounds contain various treasures such as gold necklaces, jade scarabs, rubies, black opal gemstones, sealed arcane scroll tubes with spells, and sealed perfume bottles. Additionally, there are valuable bronze doors and decorative amphorae.

IMPORTANT RULES - Excavation of the covered mounds requires 3d4+4 hours of digging with appropriate equipment (shovels, picks, etc.) purchased in town. Sealed mounds have entrances that require a sledgehammer and iron spike to break open. Opening these will necessitate a random monster roll.

LOCATIONS THAT EXIST - The named locations in the Barrow Mounds include: Sealed Mound, Plundered Mound, Collapsed Mound, Mound #7, Mound #12, Mound #16, Mound #20, Mound #27, Mound #35, Mound #36, and Mound #42.
