The Bitter Reach is a harsh, icy wilderness filled with danger and opportunity for adventurers. In this frozen land, ancient ruins of the Winter Elves lie hidden beneath the ice, waiting to be discovered by brave treasure hunters.

Key players in the Wake of the Winter King campaign include Alabastor and his Misgrown forces, as well as Ingmar, a cunning merchant with secrets of his own. The PCs may also encounter Wurda, a powerful demon causing chaos across the Bitter Reach.

Adventure awaits in the form of Ice Caves, Elven Ruins, and Settlements scattered throughout the frozen landscape. Encounters on the journey can include fierce Nanuik beasts, enigmatic Ice Giants, or even magical artifacts like Bur's Boots and Lerge's Hammer.

As the PCs explore this treacherous terrain, they'll need to rely on their wits and courage to survive the dangers that lurk around every corner. Will they uncover the secrets hidden within the ruins of the Winter Elves? Can they resist the allure of powerful magical artifacts? The choice is theirs in this frozen land of opportunity and peril.
