SETTING:
The adventure takes place in various locations surrounding the Crypt of the Mellified Mage, including Anger Bay, Backwater Bay, Maidenholm, Molden Margeleda, Yendrara Belifargrove, and others. The crypt itself is located near a village with several named establishments.

STARTING LOCATION:
The PCs can start their journey in the village near the crypt, where they may visit various shops or interact with NPCs. Some of the named establishments include the town altar, Insectoid cave, and an obscured entrance to the crypt.

KEY NPCs:
There are no specific named NPCs mentioned in the module excerpts provided.

KEY LOCATIONS:
1. The Crypt of the Mellified Mage: A honey-filled catacomb with three entrances.
2. The Village: A settlement near the crypt where settlers have built some buildings.
3. Insectoid Cave: A cave with tunnels that lead to one of the crypt's entrances.
4. Obscured Entrance: An entrance to the crypt hidden behind a stone door.

No specific rumors or hooks are mentioned in the module excerpts provided.

MAJOR TREASURES:
1. The conical caps worn by the mummified corpses in the catafalque area are worth D6 silver each.
2. The bronze clasps shaped like leathery toads found in the same area are worth 2D6 silver each.
3. Peridot shavings on the mural in the Ritual Chamber are worth a total of 2D6 silver if scratched out.
4. A topaz gem can be found inside one of the bodies in the Embalming Room, worth 4D6 silver.

IMPORTANT RULES:
1. The PCs may encounter various creatures and hazards within the crypt and surrounding areas.
2. Some weapons cause less damage against the mummified corpses than others.
3. Certain items or actions may trigger specific events or consequences within the adventure site.
